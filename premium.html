FAQ

Questions about Premium.

A quick overview of what changes with Premium, who it is for, and why annual is positioned as the preferred path.

Premium basics

Is Premium required to use Emorya?

No. The core Emorya experience remains usable without Premium. Premium is the enhanced participation layer for users who want wider access, faster progression, and the most complete version of the ecosystem.

What changes with Premium?

Premium opens up enhanced reward eligibility, faster cooldowns, all games unlocked, increased participation limits, a smoother ad-free experience, and a clearer path through the full Emorya ecosystem.

Do I need crypto experience?

No. Premium is positioned around smoother use and deeper participation, not prior crypto knowledge. Wallet-related features are still guided and can be explored when you are ready.

Why choose annual Premium?

Annual Premium is the preferred route because it offers the strongest value over time, supports uninterrupted progression, and includes automatic entry into the Premium prize draw campaign.

Can I upgrade later?

Yes. Users can begin with the standard Emorya experience and move into Premium later when they want the fuller participation layer and smoother progression path.
